Privacy Policy for Math Memo

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how nvelozsavino ("we," "us," or "our") handles information for the Math Memo mobile application. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, particularly children.

2. Information Collection

Math Memo is a local-only educational application. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you or your children. All game progress, mathematical data, and user settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

3. Permissions and Third-Party SDKs

Our app does not require access to sensitive device permissions (such as location, camera, or contacts). We do not integrate any third-party advertising or analytics SDKs that track user behavior.

4. Children’s Privacy

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy. Because our application does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, it is inherently compliant with child privacy standards. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

5. Data Retention

As we do not collect or store data externally, there is no personal data to retain, share, or delete. All data is managed solely by the user on their own device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
